Authentication

Error Codes

AUTH_EXPIRED User session expired. Trigger re-authentication flow.
AUTH_MFA_REQUIRED MFA challenge. Pause and notify user.
RATE_LIMITED Too many requests. Retry after specified delay.
SERVICE_UNAVAILABLE Upstream service down. Retry with backoff.
INVALID_INPUT Malformed parameters. Check tool schema.
